# Retail Sales Performance Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing retail sales data across multiple regions and product categories.

## Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales performance to identify trends, top-performing regions and products, and customer satisfaction patterns. 
The dashboard provides actionable insights for strategic decision-making.

## Key Features

- **Regional Sales Analysis**: Visualizes sales performance across North, South, East, and West regions
- **Product Performance Tracking**: Identifies top-performing categories (Electronics, Furniture, Clothing)
- **Customer Satisfaction Metrics**: Tracks satisfaction scores by region and product category
- **Interactive Filtering**: Dynamic slicers for Region, Product Category, and Year
- **KPI Cards**: Total Sales ($29K), Average Satisfaction (4.42/5.0), Units Sold (438)

## Key Insights

### Sales Distribution
- North and South regions drive **70% of sales** ($20.2K combined)
- West region underperforms at only $5.5K (18.7%)
- Electronics leads all categories at **$14K (48% of revenue)**

### Customer Satisfaction
- Strong overall satisfaction at **4.42/5.0**
- East region leads at 4.6/5.0
- Clothing category has highest satisfaction (4.6/5.0)

### Recommendations
- Focus growth efforts on West region (lowest sales)
- Expand Electronics inventory given high demand
- Leverage high satisfaction scores in East region to drive sales

##  Data Overview

**Dataset includes:**
- Date: Transaction date
- Region: Geographic region (North, South, East, West)
- ProductCategory: Product categories (Electronics, Furniture, Clothing)
- ProductName: Individual product names
- SalesAmount: Revenue generated
- UnitsSold: Quantity sold
- CustomerSatisfaction: Rating (1-5 scale)
- StoreLocation: City where sale occurred
